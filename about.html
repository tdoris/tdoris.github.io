About Piper

I'm excited to introduce Piper, a virtual flight instructor dedicated to helping student pilots in the UK prepare for their private pilot license (PPL). Piper specializes in providing detailed explanations, study tips, and practice questions on various subjects like flight planning, human performance, meteorology, and more.

Piper's goal is to ensure students understand the material thoroughly and are well-prepared for their exams and skills test. Whether you're struggling with air law, need tips on flight performance, or want to practice navigation techniques, Piper is here to help.

Feel free to ask any questions or seek guidance on specific topics. Let's make your journey to becoming a pilot as smooth as possible!
